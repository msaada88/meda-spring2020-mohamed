instruct running

In order to start running as a hobby, there are some key things you should do. The first would be to run or run/walk 20-30 minutes a day, two days a week. As well, it would be a good idea to take a long run or run/walk on the weekends, about 40 minutes to an hour. Ideally, you would run at a conversational pace, which means you aren't so out of breath that you can't talk to someone. Consider taking regular walk-breaks throughout your week, and on rest days, cross-train to build muscle strength
